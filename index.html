Privacy Policy – PDF Master

Last updated: April 29, 2025

PDF Master ("the app") is a free, offline utility for Android and iOS that allows users to convert images to PDF, convert PDF to images, and merge PDF files.

Data Collection

The app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. All operations are performed entirely on your device.

Permissions

  • Camera – Used only to capture photos for PDF conversion. Photos are not stored outside the app.
  • Photo Library / Storage – Used only to read images or PDF files you select and to save output files to your device.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No data is sent to any server.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL.
